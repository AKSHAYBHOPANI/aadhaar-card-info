How to download aadhaar card ? | Using aadhaar number / virtual id

June 19, 2020June 19, 2020 by akshaybhopani@gmail.com

Having an e-Aadhaar card keeps your Aadhaar information handy and eliminates the possibility that you will ever lose or forget your Aadhaar number. It is appropriate across the country as it is a digital version of your standard Aadhaar, without exceptions.

Download Via Aadhaar Number

  1. Visit the official Aadhar website https:/uidai.gov.in/
  2. Click on the My Aadhaar choice ‘Download Aadhaar’ button or visit the https:/eaadhaar.uidai.gov.in / link
  3. Choose “Aadhaar” choice under section “I Have”
  4. Enter the Aadhaar number 12-digit now. If you want to download Masked Aadhaar click on ‘I want a masked Aadhaar’ link.
  5. Enter the Captcha Verification Code and press the “Return OTP” option to get your registered mobile number with the one time password
  6. Enter the OTP your mobile number has received.
  7. Complete the survey, and press “Verify and Install” to access your Aadhaar’s electronic file

Download Via Virtual ID

  1. Visit the Indian Unique Identification Authority (UIDAI) official web site.
  2. Tap tab ‘My Aadhaar’
  3. Click on the link ‘download aadhaar’ under the tab ‘Get aadhaar’
  4. A new tab opens. Under ‘I have’ line, pick ‘Digital ID’ choice.
  5. Type your 16 digits Virtual ID number (VID) and verification code captcha as shown on the page.
  6. Click ‘OTP submit’ button.
  7. On your registered mobile phone or email address, you will obtain a one-time password (OTP)
  8. To check your request enter the OTP obtained on your mobile phone. Then click the ‘Call and Download’ button
  9. You can access your e-aadhaar card successfully in PDF format after validation, which is protected by a password.
  10. Enter the password when you open your e-aadhaar PDF document (as per the instructions given)
  11. You now have your e-aadhaar card which you can print for reference.
