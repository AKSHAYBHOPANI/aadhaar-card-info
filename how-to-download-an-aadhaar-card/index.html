How to download an Aadhaar Card ?

July 22, 2020July 22, 2020 by Pooja Totlani

A resident of India requires an Aadhaar to enjoy those social welfare services offered by the Government of India. The paper acts as a proof of address and identification for a person. Aadhaar is a 12-digit unique identification number provided by the National Identification Authority of India (UIDAI). If a person enrolls for the Aadhaar card by visiting Aadhaar Centres or Banks / Post-offices, he/she can download and print UIDAI Aadhaar by using the enrolment ID, virtual ID or Aadhaar number issued by the UIDAI. When a number is given, he/she will obey these specific steps for Aadhar card download.

Aadhar Card Download by Aadhaar Number:

 If you want to access and print e-Aadhaar card online, you need to follow the below-mentioned steps:

Step 1: Visit the Aadhar official website https:/uidai.gov.in/ 

Step 2: Click on ‘Download Aadhaar’ button from My Aadhaar page or visit the link https:/eaadhaar.uidai.gov.in/ 

Step 3: Choose the “Aadhaar” alternative under “I Have ” segment

Step 4: Now, enter the 12-digit Aadhaar Number. Tap on ‘ I want a masked Aadhaar ‘link if you want to access Masked Aadhaar. 

Step 5: Enter the Captcha Authentication Code and Press “ 

Send OTP ” option to get the one time password on your registered mobile number. 

Step 6: Insert the OTP got on your mobile number 

Step 7: Complete the survey and press “ Verify And Download ”to download an electronic copy of your Aadhaar.

 

 

 

Steps to Download e-Aadhaar Card by Virtual ID (VID):

  Downloading Aadhaar number via the Virtual ID is the latest update to UIDAI ‘s site for Aadhaar download. Follow the below-mentioned measures for Aadhaar card free download using the virtual ID online: 

Step 1: Visit UIDAI’s online portal 

Step 2: Press “ Download Aadhaar 

Step 3: Select the VID option from the “I Have ” section 

Step 4: Enter your computer ID, full name, pin code, and protection code 

Step 5: Now press “ Send OTP ” to create OTP 

Step 6: Instead, you can use the TOTP function to authenticate your request 

Step 7: e-Aadhaar will be downloaded to your machine 

Step 8: You can access it by entering the Aadhar card password.

E-Aadhar Card Download by Using Enrolment Number (EID):

In case you have not received your Aadhar card yet or have forgotten your Aadhaar number, you can still access the modified Aadhar card by entering the Aadhaar Enrolment Number (EID). Follow the given steps below for e-Aadhaar card download by enrolment number:

Step 1: Visit www.uidai.gov.in  

Step 2: Tap “ Download Aadhaar ”alternative  

Step 3: You will be routed to https://eaadhaar.uidai.gov.in/ 

Stage 4: Input the 14-digit enrolment ID number and 14-digit time and date values 

Step 5: Type your full name, pin code, image captcha code 

Step 6: Press “ Request OTP ” to create an OTP 

Step 7: Hit “ Confirm ” Button. 

Step 8: You can obtain the OTP on your registered mobile number 

Step 9: Join the OTP & click on the “ Download Aadhaar ” option

Step 10: Now you can print an electronic copy of your Aadhaar Passport. 

Steps for Aadhar Card Download by Name and Date of Birth:

In case you do not recall your Aadhaar number or EID, you can always access e-Aadhaar by entering your name and date of birth. Follow these steps for Aadhar card download:

Step 1: Visit Aadhaar website https://resident.uidai.gov.in

Step 2: Type your full name and either your registered e-mail address or mobile number and the security code

Step 3: Press the “ Send OTP ” tab

Step 4: Enter the OTP obtained on your registered mobile number and click on the “ Verify OTP ” button

Step 5: A message will appear on the screen telling the Aadhaar number is sent to your registered mobile phone

Step 6: After Finding your Aadhaar enrolment number after your smartphone, visit the e-Aadhaar page on the official UIDAI website

Step 7: Press “ I have Aadhaar ”alternative

Step 8: Enter your Aadhaar identification number, full name, pin code, captcha picture

Step 9: Click on “OTP question”

Step 10: You give an OTP to your mobile phone. Join this OTP and press the “Aadhaar now” button to access Aadhaar

How to Download e-Aadhaar from DigiLocker Account

DigiLocker has worked with the UIDAI to make the DigiLocker account with Aadhaar open to cardholders for connecting. DigiLocker is a cloud-based network for digitally releasing, processing, exchanging, and checking documents and certificates that enable the chosen registered entity to send paper or e-copies to citizens’ designated ‘digital lockers.’

To access Aadhaar from DigiLocker Account, follow the steps below to:

Step 1: Log in to the https://digilocker.gov.in/ DigiLocker account

Step 2: Press the “Sign Up” button and type your Aadhaar 12-digit number

Step 3: Press the ‘Verify’ button to ‘OTP’

Step 4: Enter the OTP your mobile number has received

Step 5: Press the ‘OTP Authentication’ button

Step 6: Shows page with ‘Issued Paper.’ Enable ‘e-Aadhaar’ with the button ‘File’
